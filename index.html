HISTORY

This Temple is Situated in Harchowal (Distt : Gurdaspur , State: Punjab) . There are two Historic Temples in the city , First is Mata JwalaMukhi Mandir and second is Shiv Mandir Baba Kanshigir G . The Shiv Mandir is Regulated by 'Baba Kanshigir Sewa Society' . Acc. to the Peoples of Harchowal , This Temple is approximately 200Years old . There are four Saints borned in Harchowal

  • Baba Haridas G
  • Baba Vega G
  • Baba Shankar G
  • Baba Kanshigir G
Acc. to the peoples of Harchowal , Baba kanshigir G was a guru of Baba Shankar G . When Baba Kanshigir G died , the peoples of Harchowal build Shiv Mandir in his Memory bacause he was a devottee of Lord Shiva .

HISTORY OF BABA KANSHIGIR G

Baba Kanshigir G was a Saint which lived in Harchowal . He was Very Powerfull Personality . Acc . to the peoples of harchowal , Lord Shiva was a guru of Baba Kanshigir g . Baba Shankar G was a disciple of Baba Kanshigir G . He was Known due to his Magic performed for the goodness of society . When , he died then the peoples of harchowal build a Lord Shiva temple in the memory of Baba G . The temple of his disciple also build near the temple of Baba G . One interesting fact about this temple is that the alcohol is used as a PRASAD in this temple but it is not compulsory to drink it . Firstly , The Family of Baba G doing all the maintenance work of the Temple but know a 12 members society was build for the maintenance of Temple .
